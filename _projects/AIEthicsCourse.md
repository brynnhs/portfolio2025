## Overview

I'm currently taking an online course on AI Ethics to deepen my understanding of responsible AI development, ethical considerations in machine learning, and the societal implications of artificial intelligence technologies.

## Course Focus Areas

- Ethical frameworks for AI development
- Bias and fairness in machine learning algorithms
- Privacy and data protection in AI systems
- Transparency and explainability in AI decision-making
- Societal impact and regulation of AI technologies

## Progress

Currently at 30% completion. Key topics covered so far:
- Introduction to AI ethics principles
- Case studies of ethical dilemmas in AI
- Frameworks for ethical AI design

## Learning Objectives

- Understand key ethical principles in AI development
- Learn to identify and mitigate bias in machine learning models
- Develop skills for building responsible AI systems
- Stay informed about current regulations and best practices

## Application

This knowledge directly applies to my work in biomedical engineering and neurotechnology, where ethical considerations are paramount when developing AI systems for healthcare applications.
